P達o de Queijo (Cheese bread rolls)

Description

This is the simplest, easiest way I've ever found to make the typical brazilian snack p達o de queijo.

Ingredients

  • 1 and a half teacups of starch (sour or manioc)
  • 200g of heavy cream
  • Salt
  • 1 teacup of grated Mozzarela cheese

Steps

  1. In a recipient, add all of the ingredients and mix them together. If the dough is too sticky, you can add more starch. The end result should still be a somewhat sticky.
  2. You can use a bit of butter on your hands to make little balls with the dough and set them aside on a tray (that has been greased). Make sure the small balls are spread apart so they don't become one when cooked (they will grow).
  3. Put the tray with the rolls in the oven at 180 degrees for around 40 minutes or until done.
